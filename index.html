I'm a research scientist at Google. I am interested in adopting and extending machine learning practices within computer graphics and vision to achieve improved and intuitive design capabilities.
In Google, I develop algorithms for intuitive image editing and creation. I am also working on improving the training and distillation of fundamental vision models.
Previously, I received my PhD under the supervision of Professor Daniel Cohen-Or and Professor Raja Giryes at the Department of Computer Science at Tel-Aviv University. During my doctoral studies, I developed models and methods for 3D shape generation, texture synthesis, 3D modeling, meshing, and more, showcasing my competence and expertise in the field.
