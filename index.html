I'm a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Tel-Aviv University under the supervision of Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or and Prof. Raja Giryes. I am interested in adopting and extending machine learning practices within computer graphics and vision. Specifically, I am studying and developing machine learning techniques for 3D representations.
